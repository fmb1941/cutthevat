Add the Cut the VAT campaign to your website

Help us share the facts and figures about the campaign with visitors to your website, by adding the following paragraph and the campaign logo.


Cut VAT now on all housing repair and renovation

Cutting VAT from the current rate of 20% to 5% for all housing renovation and repair work would benefit millions of UK households by eliminating rogue traders. The cut would help stimulate the economy and create thousands of new jobs in the construction sector. It would also help reduce the ever-increasing cost of living for families and lift many of them out of fuel poverty by making their homes more energy efficient. This is also vital in order to reduce the carbon emissions from our housing so that the UK can meet its carbon reduction commitments. Please show your support and visit the Cut the VAT campaign website to see what you can do.
